Find out how your local hospital is doing in light of COVID-19

In such uncertain times, we at Hospital Predictor would like to provide valuable crowd-sourced data about how your local hospital is doing while flattening the curve. With data at our disposal, we can keep ourselves and our community more informed.

How do we get our data?

Currently, estimated hospital occupancy rates are based on public feedback received from survey participants. We understand that crowdsourced data collection may not provide the most accurate and up-to-date statistics regarding the actual state of Canadian hospitals. We hope only to provide a rough estimate on a bigger picture.

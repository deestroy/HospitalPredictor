Find out how your local hospital is doing in light of COVID-19

In such uncertain times, we at Hospital Predictor would like to provide valuable data about how your local hospital is doing while flattening the curve. With data at our disposal we can find our sense of community and provide hospitals the space they deserve. Lets band together and come out as a better, stronger and more data-driven community. How do we get our data?

We get our data from multiple government databases as well as live updates provided by the hospital patients/staff. We combine historical data about hospital stays with up to date COVID-19 data to give the most accurate information as possible.
